🌟 Scientist of the Week

Satyendra Nath Bose

Physicist, Mathematician

Satyendra Nath Bose was an Indian physicist best known for his work on quantum mechanics in the early 1920s, providing the foundation for Bose-Einstein statistics and the theory of the Bose-Einstein condensate. His collaboration with Albert Einstein led to the recognition of bosons, a class of particles named after him.
